Pilot Rank System

Our rank system is designed to simulate real airline progression. As you gain experience and complete flights, you unlock new aircraft, routes, and responsibilities. Each rank represents your growth as a professional pilot within SpiceJet Virtual.

★

Trainee

You are a new pilot and will fly domestic routes with the 737 MAX 8.

★

Cadet

More domestic routes are now unlocked.

★

Junior First Officer

You have now unlocked the turboprop Dash-8 aircraft.

★

Senior First Officer

You can now fly international routes.

★

Captain

You can now fly the Boeing 737-900ER.

★

Senior Captain

You can now fly all routes and operate every aircraft.

★

Chief Pilot

You can now lead and manage a specific flight route.

★

Elite Pilot

You have become the legendary SpiceJet Virtual pilot and hold one of the highest authorities.
